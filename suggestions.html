Flood Protection for Vehicles

Vehicle Protection Suggestions

Parking in Safe Areas

Always park your vehicle in a high elevation area if you are aware of an impending flood. Use the app to find the nearest high ground within a 2-kilometer radius.

Using Physical Barriers

Consider placing sandbags or bricks around your vehicle's tires to elevate it slightly and create a barrier against shallow floodwaters.

Vehicle Covers

Use a waterproof vehicle cover to protect against rain and floodwaters. Ensure that the cover is securely fastened to prevent it from being dislodged by strong winds or flowing water.

Emergency Preparedness

Keep an emergency kit in your vehicle, including essential items such as a first aid kit, flashlight, and water purification tablets. This kit can be crucial if you are stranded in your vehicle during a flood.

Vehicle Elevation Devices

For added protection, consider using vehicle elevation devices like portable ramps or jack stands to lift your vehicle above ground level temporarily.
